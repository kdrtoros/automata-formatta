# Automata Formatta

**Intelligent Academic Manuscript Audit & Formatting Tool**

Evaluate your academic manuscripts against APA, Harvard, Chicago, MLA, and IEEE formatting standards. Detect statistical symbols, normalize spacing, and export publication-ready documents.

## Features

- ✅ **Multi-standard support:** APA 7th, Harvard, Chicago, MLA 9th, IEEE
- ✅ **Smart detection:** Identifies M, SD, p, t, F, n, N, df, SE, r, z, U, χ², η², d, ηp², R²
- ✅ **Variable tracking:** Detects Greek letters and algebraic variables
- ✅ **Automatic formatting:** Normalizes spacing, enforces leading zeros, manages indentation
- ✅ **File upload:** Drag & drop .docx or .txt files
- ✅ **Export options:** Copy to clipboard or download as .doc
- ✅ **Privacy-first:** 100% browser-based — no data sent to servers
- ✅ **Interactive UI:** Real-time audit status, manuscript health metrics

## How It Works

1. **Select a citation standard** (APA, Harvard, Chicago, MLA, or IEEE)
2. **Paste your manuscript text** or upload a .docx file
3. **Click "Audit & Process"** to analyze formatting
4. **Review the health report** (perfect format or adjustments applied)
5. **Copy or download** your cleaned manuscript

## What Gets Checked

| Category | What It Detects |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Spacing** | Normalizes whitespace around = < > operators |
| **Statistics** | Identifies M, SD, p, t, F, n, N, df, SE, r, z, U, χ², η², d, ηp², R² |
| **Variables** | Detects variable names and Greek letters (α, β, γ, etc.) |
| **Formatting** | Enforces leading zero rules, line spacing, font, indentation per style |

## Citation Standards

| Standard | Font | Spacing | Indent | Zero Rule |
|----------|------|---------|--------|-----------|
| **APA 7th** | Times New Roman | 2.0 | 0.5" | Omit (p = .05) |
| **Harvard** | Arial | 1.5 | 0" | Include (p = 0.05) |
| **Chicago** | Times New Roman | 1.5 | 0.5" | Include |
| **MLA 9th** | Times New Roman | 2.0 | 0.5" | Omit |
| **IEEE** | Times New Roman | 1.0 | Justify | Include |
